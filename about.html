About Me


I graduated from the University of Virginia in 2016 with a degree in Economics. What I enjoyed most was learning about elegant approaches to using data to tell compelling stories.


A great story obviously doesn't need data to be meaningful. As a big film fan, I don't need to think very hard to come up with examples. But a good story with data? That's the best way to influence people.


It even happened to me when I started to keep track where my time went during the day. No big shocker: I spend a lot of time on my computer.


However, that insight led me to discover my passion for learning about elegant approaches to building beautiful web applications. I'm looking forward to telling some compelling stories of my own. Cheers!



Contact Me with potential projects, cool data visualizations, or to talk about the lastest Linklater movie. It'll be fun.
