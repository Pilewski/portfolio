About Me


I graduated from the University of Virginia in 2016 with a degree in Economics. What I enjoyed learning about most was how elegant approaches to using data can be used to tell compelling stories.


A great story obviously doesn't need data to be meaningful, but a great story with data can change people.


It happened to me. I started observing my behaviors a little closer in an effort to lead a more deliberate life. It didn't take long for me to make the stunning insight that I spent a lot of time on the internet...


So before long I decided I had to learn about the elegant approaches to creating it! I'm looking forward to telling some compelling stories of my own.



Contact Me with potential projects, interesting data visualizations, or anything else. It'll be fun.
